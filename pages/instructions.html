General Instructions

Please try to provide correct details in contact form , it will be convenient for me to contact you back.

you can use your twitter or facebook username in email option. You need to mention like this :
fb@<username>
twitter@<username>
where <username> is specific to you.

All fields in contact form are required. As you fill a correct detail in contact form its text and border changes from orange to Teal.
Teal colour indicates that detail provided is valid and can be accepted in contact form. Invalid detail remains within orange text and border and couldn't be processed further.

Click on that paper plane button to proceed whenever you have filled all valid details. You will get a Google Captcha page to complete form , its just a verification to prevent spam and bot messages. Complete the captcha and you are done!

Once, your form has been submitted. You can expect your reply within few hours ! However, It may be possible that i'm busy with my schedule and couldn't reply back to you in expected time.
I'm usually free in weekends so you'll surely get your reply in weekend days.
